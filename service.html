Paw-sibilities: Our Service in Bone-fide Detail

Free Consultation:

I provide a free 30 to 45-minute introductory session, providing an opportunity for me to acquaint myself with both you and your canine companion. This personalized session takes place in the comfort of your home, where we embark on a leisurely stroll together. During our time together, we'll delve into your unique requirements and assess the suitability of my services for your furry friend.
Additionally, I offer an opportunity for us to review my terms and conditions collaboratively. This ensures transparency and establishes clear expectations for both parties, allowing us to address any potential concerns that may arise.

Dog Walking Solo:

Each dog possesses unique needs and preferences. Some dogs may prefer solitary walks due to their shyness, antisocial tendencies, or occasional anxiety in group settings. This is perfectly normal, and I provide individualized solo walks tailored to these preferences. During these walks, it will be just the dog and me, ensuring a comfortable and enjoyable experience for your furry friend.

Dog Walking Group:

Is your pet a social butterfly? Are you eager to introduce your dog to new furry friends? With this service, I lead groups of dogs on interactive walks, providing opportunities for them to meet other dogs in the neighborhood. They can engage in playful interactions, fostering positive social skills and fun-filled moments together.

Home Visits:

This service is tailored to provide dogs with a refreshing break, a light snack, playtime, and dedicated attention. It proves especially advantageous for pet owners who spend extended hours away from their beloved furry friends. I will visit your home to carry out the pet care tasks we discussed during our consultation. Please note that pricing can be customized to accommodate individual needs and preferences

Adventure Day:

Give your dog the special day they deserve with my Adventure Day service. Treat them to a full day of adventure, which includes up to four hours of exciting activities. Destinations may vary and can include canal walks with doggy companions, delightful pub pit stops featuring sausages and refreshing drinks, invigorating beach walks with dog-friendly ice cream and swimming, and serene woodland strolls with a picnic that includes tasty treats for your furry friend.
Please note that dogs participating in these trips should be well-socialized with reliable recall, as they will be part of groups in public places.

Ramble Time:

Enjoy 1.5-2 hours of group activities, exploring woodlands, farmer's fields, and secure areas on occasion. This option is ideal for dogs who thrive in a group setting, possess high energy levels, and excellent social skills. This offering is available once a week, subject to availability. Please feel free to reach out for further discussion and to confirm details

Dog/ Pet Sitting and Drop in Services:

Planning a getaway? Or perhaps you're heading out of town for a few days and can't take your beloved pup with you? Whatever the occasion, I offer in-home dog sitting services while you're away. This way, your furry friend can enjoy the comforts of home and the company of a familiar face. Specific requirements will be addressed during our consultation
